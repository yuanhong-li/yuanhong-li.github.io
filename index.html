Overview

I am a PhD Candidate of School of Cyber Science and engineering at Huazhong University of Science and Technology, under the supervision of Prof. Peng Xu. I am interested in applied cryptography, particularly in how cryptographic systems to achieve credibility and privacy in real-world. The work I'm completing encompasses formal modeling of cryptographic primitives, designing and analyzing cryptosystems with provable security, and benchmarking performance.
